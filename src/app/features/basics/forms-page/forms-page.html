Forms in Angular

Angular forms are used to collect, validate, and manage user input efficiently.

What are Forms in Angular?

Angular provides two major approaches for handling forms: 1. Template-Driven Forms 2. Reactive Forms Template-driven forms are simpler and ideal for smaller forms, while Reactive Forms are scalable and powerful for enterprise applications.
